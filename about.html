About Me

Hi! I'm Tala, a passionate photographer and graphic designer (as well as web developer but that's not the point of this portfolio). I specialize in photographing nature, animals, people, but mainly skylines, buildings, and honestly, anything with a sunset. My goal is to capture moments that tell stories and create lasting memories. Whether it's nature, portraits, or urban scenes, I strive to bring unique perspectives to every shot.

As for graphic design, I mainly specialize in digital design and content creation, and have designed a variety of posts, newsletters, and pamphlets. I have always been passionate about graphic design, as it allows me to blend creativity with functionality, crafting visually stunning pieces that communicate powerful messages.
